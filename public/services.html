Services

Mold Testing - $350

Our mold testing process begins with a brief consultation, followed by three air sample tests. Whether those air samples are wall cavity samples, surface samples, or bulk depends on the judgement of the inspector, as well as the property owner. Antigen will collect the air samples upon your property, and then send those results to our lab. The base amount of samples for the lab to get an accurate reading is three, that is why our base package comes with three samples included!

Additional Charges Include
  • additional samples - $75
  • travel (outside harris county or surrounding counties) - $50-$100
  • overtime (greater than 2 hrs on site. M-Fr after 5pm) - $50/hr
  • cancelation - $120
  • weekend/holiday premium - $100

**Please note commercial properties tend to take 5+ air samples**

Mold Inspection - $200

Our mold Inspection (or remediation protocol) is a thorough written report of our recomendations for any third party vendors to follow throughout the remediation process. Our Inspection process consists of a visual inspection, relative humidity (RH) readings, moisture readings of construction materials, and an inspection report with our recommendations. Texas mold laws state that when professional mold remediation is required, we must provide a safe protocol for removal and disposal of materials. Delivery of the report depends on the turn around time of the lab results, but our experienced average is 7 days.

**Please note any recommendations made to third party remediation companies are made because those companies have provided positive results in the past.**
