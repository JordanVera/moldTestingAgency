About Antigen

Why Us

At Antigen, we have our methods, and those methods work. By only offering mold testing, we can give our customers an unbiased opinion. This ensures that an honest service is provided, as well as a premium service. All of our professional mold inspectors are licensed through the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation and are also held to a professional company policy. By specializing in the testing process only, Antigen strives to provide elaborate tests and deliberate remediation protocols. This means that our customers never have to worry about a remediation company over-charging them for an unnecessary reconstruction. By getting an external vendor to run tests, it offers our customers assurance that the remediation process isn't needless or extraneous.

Source of Mold Guarantee

We guarantee to focus on finding the source of the mold growth during our inspection, and provide the appropriate recommendations to properly address the situation to assure that there won't be a reoccurring mold problem once the remediation has been performed. We use the most current and advanced technology and the most modern equipment, and we regularly update our techniques to insure that our customers get the most comprehensive inspection report.
