Our Company

Our philosophy is simple. By not offering remediation services, we provide our customers with unbiased mold tests and protocols for any third party remediation companies to follow. Our methods allow us to conduct business without any conflict of interests, that way you never over-pay for mold repair services. All of our mold inspectors and consultants are licensed through the state of Texas. Our mold tests will verify any mold growth, upon a positive test result, we recommend a full mold inspection of the property. In fact, Texas laws state that a mold inspection report must be drawn up if professional remediation services are needed. Depending on the scope of the project, a mold test might not be needed, more on that here. We offer mold testing services to both commercial and residential properties.

Antigen is a test-only agency so we always have our customers best interests in mind!
